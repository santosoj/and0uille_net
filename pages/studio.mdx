and0uille endorses:

[OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/download#linux) screen recording software

[Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) free audio editor

[FFmpeg](https://ffmpeg.org/) cross-platform video cauldron

[PaulXStretch](https://sonosaurus.com/paulxstretch/) extreme time-stretching

[GIMP](https://www.gimp.org/) GNU image manipulation program

[ExifTool](https://exiftool.org/) meta information editor

That's all anybody will ever need.
